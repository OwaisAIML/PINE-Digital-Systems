FAQ

Answers to common questions.

Still unsure? You can always DM on Instagram or fill the form below. We’ll reply with honest recommendations — even if we’re not the right fit.

Every project is different, but most client work falls into one of these categories:

  • Launch a new website or landing page
  • Automate an existing manual workflow (DMs, orders, follow-ups)
  • Add an AI assistant to handle common customer questions
  • Create a simple internal dashboard for operations

If your idea is more complex, we can start with a “minimum viable system” and expand over time.

How much does a typical project cost?
It depends on the scope. Simple landing pages or small automations cost less, full systems cost more. After a quick call, you’ll get a clear fixed-price quote — no surprise fees.
Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes. Everything can be handled online with Loom videos, chat and a shared dashboard. Payments can be set up in INR or international options depending on the project.
What tools / tech do you use?
A mix of modern web tech (HTML, CSS, JS, frameworks), AI models, automation tools, and APIs — always chosen based on what fits your use case and budget instead of what’s “trendy”.
Can you maintain and update systems after launch?
Yes. You can choose a one-time project only, or add a light monthly plan where we monitor, update and keep everything running smoothly as your business grows.
